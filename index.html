Key Finding

Four authors act as the main bridges in the TR network. Erkan Saka and Christine Ogan are doubly critical since they bridge different research domains internally and connect the TR network to global research hubs through CSS co-authorship. Mustafa Öz and Umut Korkut function as bridges solely within the TR citation network through concept importation.
